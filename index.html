Managing My Stress in the Pandemic

Feeling sad, stressed, confused, scared or angry during a crisis is normal. Enable for me to avoid those, I always talk to the people whom I can trust such as my family and friends at home and online. Aside from that, I always maintain a healthy lifestyle and exercise to become healthy and reduce the risk of getting sick.

Despite the challenges we are all facing now due to COVID-19, we can still find purpose in our lives and be supportive of one another. When faced with a difficult situation, focus on what you can change, accept matters that are beyond your control and always strive to live by your values.
